# BlackStronghold — $600 Digital Starter Pack: Sales Playbook

Target: owner-operated trade shops (welding, machine shops, auto repair) with **no website**.
Source: `output/HOT_leads_only.csv` — 157 leads, 147 with phone numbers.
Offer: one-page website + AI receptionist (WhatsApp/SMS) + Google Business profile setup. **$600 flat, live in under a week.**

---

## The 30-second cold call opener

> "Hi, is this the owner of [SHOP NAME]?
>
> My name is [NAME] — I'll be quick because I know you're working.
> I found your shop on Google. You've got [X] reviews and a [4.X] rating — people clearly like your work. But you have **no website**, which means every time someone searches '[trade] near me', your competitors show up and you don't.
>
> Here's why I'm calling: my company builds websites with a receptionist built in — an assistant that answers your phone messages and WhatsApp **24/7**, gives quotes, and books jobs **while your hands are busy working**.
>
> It's $600, one time, everything included, live in under a week. Before you say anything — can I text you a link right now so you can see exactly what your site would look like? Takes you 30 seconds to look."

**Goal of call #1 is NOT the sale.** It's permission to send the demo link. The demo closes; you just open the door.

---

## The advantages (your ammo — use 2-3 per call, not all)

1. **Missed calls are lost jobs.** "When you're under a truck or behind the torch and the phone rings, where does that customer go? To the next shop on the list. Your AI answers in 2 seconds, every time, even at 11pm on a Sunday."

2. **You're invisible where customers are looking.** "78% of people check a business online before calling. Right now, when they search for you — nothing. Your competitor with a worse rating but a website gets the call."

3. **Your reviews are wasted.** "You already earned [X] Google reviews. They're sitting on a Maps listing nobody scrolls. We put them front and center on your own site, working for you."

4. **It speaks your customers' language.** "The assistant answers in English AND Spanish — automatically, depending on the customer." *(Lead with this in El Paso, Laredo, San Antonio, Miami.)*

5. **It works where your customers already are: WhatsApp.** "No app to download, nothing to learn. Customers message your number like they already do — the difference is now somebody always answers."

6. **One price, no surprises.** "$600 once. Site, domain, the AI receptionist, your Google profile cleaned up. No monthly fees to start, no contracts."

7. **Speed.** "You're live before next [DAY-OF-WEEK]. I'm not selling you a 3-month project."

## Objection handling

**"I get enough work from word of mouth."**
> "That's exactly why I called you and not a dead shop. Word of mouth got you [X] reviews. The site turns that same word of mouth into more jobs — when your customer recommends you, the first thing their friend does is look you up. Right now they find nothing."

**"$600 is a lot."**
> "What's one job worth to you — a transmission, a custom gate, a fab job? $300? $500? This pays for itself the first or second job it catches. And it catches the ones you're missing right now."

**"My nephew/son can make me a website."**
> "And he should — for free, take it. But has he built one with an assistant that answers your WhatsApp at midnight and books the job? That's the part that makes money. The website is the face; the receptionist is the worker."

**"I don't understand the AI stuff."**
> "You don't have to. Your customers text your number, it answers like a good employee, you get the job in your hand booked. If it ever says something wrong, you have the final word — every conversation is visible to you."

**"Let me think about it."**
> "Of course. Did you get the link I sent? Take a look tonight — that site is yours, I built it for you either way. I'll call you [DAY]. If you don't want it, no hard feelings, I'll take it down."
> *(Scarcity, honest version: demos get taken down after 2 weeks.)*

## The close

> "Look, here's how simple this is: you say yes today, you send me your logo or just the name you want on it, and by [DAY] customers are getting answered while you work. $600, half now half when it's live if you prefer. Do you want it in English and Spanish, or just English?"

*(The last question is an assumptive close — they answer the language question, they've bought.)*

## Pricing structure & the real money

| | |
|---|---|
| **Starter Pack** | $600 one-time: site + AI receptionist + Google Business setup |
| **Payment option** | 50% start / 50% on delivery (lowers the barrier) |
| **Upsell (month 2+)** | $49–99/mo: AI receptionist hosting, updates, monthly report of conversations/jobs caught |
| **The endgame** | The monthly plan is the actual business — 20 shops × $79/mo = $1,580 MRR recurring, and that's the SaaS |

**Rule:** never lead with the monthly. Sell the $600 pack, deliver fast, then show them the report of what the AI answered — the upsell sells itself.
